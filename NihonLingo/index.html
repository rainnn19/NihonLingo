✨ Word of the Day

Thank you

ありがとう
(Arigatou)

Pronunciation: ah-ree-gah-toh

A common expression of gratitude used in everyday situations.

Good luck

頑張って
(Ganbatte)

Pronunciation: gahn-bah-teh

Used to encourage someone or show support before a challenge.

Wonderful

素晴らしい
(Subarashii)

Pronunciation: soo-bah-rah-shee

Expresses admiration or praise for something exceptional.
