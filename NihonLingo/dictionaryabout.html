🚀 How to Use Nihonlingo Dictionary

🔍 Searching for Words

Use the search bar at the top of the dictionary page to find specific words. You can search in:

  • English: Type "hello" to find the Japanese equivalent
  • Japanese: Type "こんにちは" to find the English meaning
  • Romaji: Type "konnichiwa" for pronunciation-based search

📖 Browsing Alphabetically

Click on any letter in the alphabet navigation to jump directly to that section. This is perfect for systematic learning or when you want to explore related words.

📱 Mobile Learning

Access Nihonlingo on your phone or tablet for learning on the go. The responsive design ensures all features work perfectly on any screen size.

💡 Japanese Learning Tips

🎯 Start with Basics

Begin with essential words like greetings, numbers, and common objects. Master these before moving to complex vocabulary.

🔊 Practice Pronunciation

Pay attention to the romaji and pronunciation guides. Japanese has different sounds than English, so practice regularly.

📝 Use Flashcards

Create flashcards with the Japanese characters on one side and English on the other. Review regularly to build memory.

🎬 Watch Japanese Media

Watch anime, dramas, or listen to Japanese music while using Nihonlingo to look up unfamiliar words.

💬 Practice Daily

Consistency is key! Try to learn 5-10 new words each day and review previous ones regularly.

🌸 Learn Culture Too

Understanding Japanese culture helps with language learning. Words often carry cultural context and meaning.
